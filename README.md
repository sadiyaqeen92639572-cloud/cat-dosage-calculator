# Cat Medication Dosage Calculator by Weight

**[→ Open the Live Calculator](https://sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/cat-dosage-calculator/)**

Free interactive tool to calculate common cat medication dosages by weight. Enter your cat's weight in lbs or kg and get instant dosage ranges for the most prescribed feline medications — with safety warnings specific to cats.

## Medications Covered

- **Gabapentin** — pre-vet anxiety ("the cat calmer"), nerve pain, seizures
- **Clavamox (Amoxicillin-Clavulanate)** — bacterial infections, skin, dental, respiratory
- **Cerenia (Maropitant)** — vomiting, nausea, motion sickness, post-surgical nausea
- **Prednisolone** — inflammation, allergies, IBD, immune-mediated conditions
- **Onsior (Robenacoxib)** — acute pain, post-operative pain (cats cannot use most NSAIDs)
- **Methimazole** — hyperthyroidism management
- **Metronidazole** — digestive issues, diarrhea, inflammatory bowel disease
- **Buprenorphine** — post-surgical pain, moderate-to-severe acute pain

## How to Use

1. Go to **[sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/cat-dosage-calculator/](https://sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/cat-dosage-calculator/)**
2. Enter your cat's weight (lbs or kg)
3. Select the medication
4. Read the recommended dosage range with feline-specific safety notes

Dosage ranges are based on standard feline veterinary references. Always confirm with your vet before administering any medication.

## Why Cats Need Their Own Calculator

Cats are not small dogs. Many medications safe for dogs are toxic to cats (Acetaminophen, Aspirin, most NSAIDs). This calculator only includes medications with established feline safety profiles, and flags critical warnings — like the retinal toxicity cap on Enrofloxacin — that generic dog dosage charts miss entirely.
